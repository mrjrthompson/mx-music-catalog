WELCOME!

The Catalog of Mexican Music for the Oboe is the product of over four years of doctoral research. This is meant to be a resource for the oboe community to use to expand repertoire for performance and study. This collection contains both solo and chamber music for the oboe up to 10 instruments. The catalog is listed alphabetically by composer last name, but search parameters allow for instrumentation queries. Up to date, this is the most comprehensive collection of Mexican music for the oboe. I welcome composers, scholars, and performers to contribute to this catalog. Please fill out a form in the menu above to contribute a work. Recordings are featured in the Discography. New works are featured on the homepage, so please visit often!
